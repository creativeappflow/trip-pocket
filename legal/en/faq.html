Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Do I need to record a currency exchange before adding expenses?

Trip Pocket is a travel account book based on exchanged amounts. Cash and debit card expenses are deducted from your exchange balance, so exchange records are required. However, credit card expenses are not deducted from your exchange balance, so they can be recorded without any exchange records.

What is a weighted average exchange rate?

When you exchange currency multiple times, the rate differs each time. The weighted average rate is calculated using each exchange amount as a weight. For example, if you exchanged $1,000 at 900 and $500 at 920, the weighted average would be (1000x900 + 500x920) / 1500 = 906.67.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device, and country/currency information is built into the app. You can use all features without an internet connection.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored only on your device (locally). Nothing is sent to external servers. If you delete the app, all data will be deleted as well.

What features are available after ending a trip?

When you end a trip, a trip report is automatically generated. You can view total expenses, category breakdowns, payment method summaries, and more. You can also export the report as an image or CSV. The settlement feature for splitting costs between participants will be added in a future update.

Can I track cash and card balances separately?

Yes. When recording an exchange, you can enter cash and debit card amounts separately, and each balance is tracked independently. Credit card expenses do not affect the balance.

Can I back up my data?

Cloud backup is not available yet. After ending a trip, you can export your report as an image or CSV to keep a record. Cloud backup will be available in a future update.

What languages are supported?

We support 7 languages: Korean, English, Japanese, Spanish, Portuguese, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese. You can change the language in the Settings screen.
